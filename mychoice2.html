My Favorite Summer and Winter Activities

Things to do in Summer Season

There are tons of exciting activities for teens to do in the summer. Below are a few of my favorites.

☆Go on a trail bike ride ☆Water balloon battle
☆Create a trampoline fort ☆Backyard camping
☆Watch movies at a drive-in ☆Go golfing/mini golfing
☆Go to a beach/lake ☆Drive random back roads and see where you end up

Things to do in Winter Season

There are also plenty of things for teens to do in the winter with some free time. Below are my favorite things to do in the winter when the weather is cool some days, and frigid on others.

☆Go sledding ☆Build a snowman
☆Make sugar cookies and decorate them ☆Walk trails or parks
☆Binge-watch christmas classics ☆Make home-made hot chocolate
☆Try ice skating/skiing/ snowboarding ☆Rate house's christmas lights
